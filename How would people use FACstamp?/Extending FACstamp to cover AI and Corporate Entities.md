Only human beings can obtain a FACstamp license and a [FACID](https://docs.google.com/document/u/0/d/1YVqu2WcwWs4DcwRit71nmY71KicRNEKGxInupWymlyE/edit), and both are required to create FACstamps.  Bots, software and AI programs, business, corporate, and government entities cannot FACstamp content.  Given this restriction as well as the various FACstamp software license restrictions, additional use cases can come into play.
## aiFACstamps

FACIDs and FACstamps are limited to human beings.  To be sentient and conscious is a requirement for the ability to assess historical accuracy of content as well as its virtuous delivery.  Bots and software programs, AI based or otherwise, and businesses, corporations, and governments cannot obtain either FACIDs or FACstamps.

Regarding AI models, what if the officers of the company producing the AI model were able to combine their personal FACstamps to create a special generative AI model FACstamp, [[aiFACstamp]]?  The generative FACstamp would furnish two important guardrails regarding generative AI Content:

1) A generative AI FACstamp would provide a liability barrier between the generative AI model end-users, who may be FACID owners, and the officers of the company.  Limiting such liability would support greater possibilities for investment and innovation.  
2) A generative AI FACstamp would provide a provenance and fitness-for-use of the content being generated and consumed.  As artificial intelligence is not the same thing as consciousness, there is significant social and civic interest for the ability to determine the fitness-for-use of generative AI content.  As FACID and FACstamp information is both ‘live’ and versioned via public ledger technology similar to blockchain technologies, generative AI as well as its end-users can more deeply investigate FACID and FACstamp provenance and hence provenance in general.  
   
In summary a generative AI FACstamp could be of high value to society.
## cFACstamps
Similar to aiFACstamps, [[cFACstamp]] would allow a corporate or governmental entity to gain a pseudo FACstamp with limitations.  Like the aiFACstamp, the cFACstamp would be directly connected to the FACIDs and FACstamps of the owners of the cFACstamp but unlike corporate common law, there would not be a separation of certain data and liabilities.
