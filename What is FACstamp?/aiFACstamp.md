An aiFACstamp is an extension of the FACstamp concept to include AI, non-human, generated digital content.  One or more FACID and FACstamp license owners can decide to sign an aiFACstamp license agreement.  This agreement extends the FACstamp fitness-for-use warranty program with limitations in both warranty and liability regarding the AI generated content.

An aiFACstamp allows AI generated content to also offer a [[Express Warranty of Fitness-For-Use|Firness-for-Use]] expressed warranty with adjusted limits of liability and coverage, providing a degree of liability protection of the FACTID and FACstamp owner(s) from AI generated content.  Regardless, only humans can obtain a FACID, sign a FACstamp, and thus ultimately sign a aiFACstamp.

Like a cFACstamp, aiFACstamps allow for a buffer layer regarding warranty claims.  However, an aiFACstamp like a cFACstamp is only a buffer - if the aiFACstamp entity does not honor a warranty claim, the claim can transfer through to the FACIDs behind the aiFACstamp.

And similar to a cFACstamp, claims against the backing FACID and FACstamps are visible through the aiFACstamp.
