FACstamp is a cryptographic based communication and social networking service that supports trustable, non duplicitous human communication via digital content

Facstamp:
- Directly facilitates a cultural shift towards more fair and responsible, more accurate and non duplicitous, digital content - FACstamp counters [[Cognitive Theft]] 
- Creates a cryptographic fitness-for-use warranty program supporting a simple and easy method of creating and tracking fitness-for-use digital content warranty claims
- Creates a secure and private internet user identity that supports both anonymous and identified use that is uniquely tied to voter registration name and address

FACstamp combines aspects of the following seven diverse technology and information domains into one integrated digital content ecosystem:
- Identity management similar to Clear® and other similar regulatory identity systems
- Quality and providence controls analogous to the Underwriters Laboratory Seal and Good Housekeeping Seal of Approval but with cryptographic security
- Wikipedia like content and reference management
- Braver Angel / Non Violent Communication conflict resolution and arbitration
- Individual and collective growth in wisdom path traditions of choice at scale
- Open source public ledger technology
- Copyright and additional stringent and litigable software license agreements
