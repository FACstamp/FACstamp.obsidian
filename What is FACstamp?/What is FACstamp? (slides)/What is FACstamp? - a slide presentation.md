## FACstamp is:
- Open source software and cryptographic technology
- An integrated set of license and warranty agreements that supply a fitness-for-use warranty on digital content
- A distributed, uncentralized, multi-path method of conflict resolution
- A new and effective way to create value for curated digital content, disambiguating valuable and valueless content, creating new markets
