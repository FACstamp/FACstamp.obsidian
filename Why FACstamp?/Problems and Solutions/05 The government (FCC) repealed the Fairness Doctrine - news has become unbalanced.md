In 1987 the FCC and President Reagon abolished the [Fairness Doctrine](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fairness_doctrine), which allowed the unrestrained rise of vicious, unvirtuous, and unbalanced demagoguery as news.  FACstamp brings the Fairness Doctrine back into existence in a more First Amendment friendly and voluntary manner by focusing on the ethical and unbiased intent of any creator and content, not just FCC controlled airspace.  In addition the arbitration is accomplished in a private, distributed, and nongovernmental manner while aligning it more closely with modern social media and online content practices and norms.
