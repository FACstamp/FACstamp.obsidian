As powerful and beneficial science can be, science can also do great harm.  In FACstamped Content is important to incorporation weaponization by simplification and remove the nuance between good and bad science.  Science can be bad in two fundamental ways.  Bad science can simply be unscientific \- bad science does not follow the [scientific method](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scientific_method), is not transparent, and can contain [confirmation bias](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Confirmation_bias) as well as other biases.

As scientific studies can be good or bad even after peer review and publication, FACstamp helps both the general public and the scientific community (all Content Consumers) helps track the current state of scientific accuracy of FACstamped Content via the FACstamp public ledger and the always live FIT and K/S status.
