FACID:
- Provides a warranty of ‘human-ness’ & uniqueness
- No corporations, bots, AI programs, governments, …
- Tied to (potential) voting registration
- Maintains privacy limits and security requirements
- Registration location is blurred though verifiable
- Name/ID can be validated with or without anonymity
- Can create/share verifiable tokens with or without anonymity
