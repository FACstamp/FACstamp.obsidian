FACstamp:
- Leverages a Public Ledger
- Implements an authenticated public/private identity tied to a voter’s (potential) registered name and address (FACID)
- Cryptographically signs digital content maintaining live FACstamp and FACID status
- See [[What is FACstamp?]]

A key technical aspect of all blockchain implementations is the concept of a public ledger that insures history - the past cannot be altered unnoticed.  Insuring the past is a fundamental tool for combating fascism - it breaks Orwellian universes.
