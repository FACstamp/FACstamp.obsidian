There are three primary legal roles concerning FACstamp:  the Licensor, a Licensee, and a Consumer. 

The FACstamp Licensor is the FACstamp trademark, copyright, and patent owner, nominally a 501(c) non profit.  A FACstamp Licensee is person who has signed and has been accepted as both a FACstamp and FACID software license agreement.  A FACstamp Consumer is someone who has signed and has an accepted and currently valid FACID. 

A FACstamp Licensee is a person who can create and place a FACstamp seal on digital content.  A FACstamp Consumer is an active FACID holder who can create and participate in FACstamp Fitness-For-Use warranty claims.
