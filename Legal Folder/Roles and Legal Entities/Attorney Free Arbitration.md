Note that FACstamp warranty claim arbitration is nominally an attorney free arbitration process. One primary goal of FACstamp is increase the effect radius of kindness and understanding, increasing consciousness, of all parties, more than just FACstamp Licensees and Consumers.  And critical aspect of achieving this goal is to minimize the overhead and complexity of the warranty claim process.  

As such the warranty claim process is designed to not require licensed attorneys and instead be based on temporary arbitration charrette guided by conflict resolution experts, such as NVC and braver angle experts. The arbitration process is geared to assisting the participants, content producers and consumers, to come together in kindness in the mutual pursuit of the veritas in question.
