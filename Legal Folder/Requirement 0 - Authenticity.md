The first requirement of a FACstamp is the authenticity of authorship of the content - content provenance.  The Internet is based on anonymity at many levels.  The first requirement is to be able to validate with high confidence in an independent and repeatable manner that the content of interest has been authored by a specific human being.

To accomplish this, a set of asymmetric cryptographic keys are created once a real identity verification has been achieved.  These keys comprise a FACID.

FACID’s are the layer that provides the authenticity of FACstamp Licensees and FACstamp Consumers.  FACID’s associate the person who signs a FACstamp License agreement with a unique online identity.
