At the moment there is a great need for project feedback.  We really could use general or specific usage feedback, particularly regarding how FACstamp could/should be used by the average end user.  The average end user is someone without a FACID or a FACstamp license - such users are just average digital content consumers searching for and consuming digital content.

The project has already received preliminary technical and legal feedback, all of which has been positive and project affirming.  To continue those conversations the various usage workflows and in particular the warranty claim process need to be more thought out, documented, and detailed.

Once we have four to six volunteers willing to put in approximately 40-80 hours each, we will convene a series of design charrettes to work out these use cases in more detail.  And then go back for a deeper tech and legal review.

Until then, please consider offering feedback either by starting [GitHub Discussion](https://github.com/FACstamp/FACstamp.obsidian/discussions/categories/general) or reaching out via this [email relay](mailto:97.striped.aerator@icloud.com).
